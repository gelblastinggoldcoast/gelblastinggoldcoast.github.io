Gel Blasting Gold Coast

Gel blasting is a great passtime

Gel blasting is commonly labelled as 'gang fighting' with something that barely qualifies as not being a gun. The media has pinned it down as a violent, dangerous and unethical practice performed by those who are a threat to society. However, this isn't actually true.

Gel blasting is the perfect thing for you and your friends to do in your spare time. It encourages teamwork and strengthens bonds with those you go with. It isn't the dark and sketchy activity it is often portrayed as, but really an enjoyable passtime, using harmless guns that are legally classified as toys. If you want a dangerous sport, then realistically, gel blasting is the last place you'd go.
