Locations

Where to go

If you don't have private property that isn't in plain view of the public, or you want to play at a proper field, then you need somewhere to go and do gel blasting.
There are many locations around Australia that allow you to gel blast, and many of them are of very high standard. Click here to see some of the places you can go on Google Maps.

The best place to go in Gold Coast

The best Gold Coast indoor field available is provided by Gelsoft Australia, and is located at 72 Link Drive, Yatala. Click here to see the route to this field from your location.

The field at 72 Link Drive also has a shop right next door. It has a huge variety of gel blasters at competitive prices. If you want to start your hobby in gel blasting, then Link Drive is as far as you'll ever need to go.
